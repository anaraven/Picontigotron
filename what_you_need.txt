Here is the list of what is needed to construct the picontigotron :

 - A box
 - A Raspberry Pi (version B in the original model)
 - A Standard LCD screen 16x2     (http://www.adafruit.com/products/181)
 - A Mini Thermal Receipt Printer (http://www.adafruit.com/product/597)
 - 3 Push Buttons
 - 1 I/O button
 - 2 LEDs (red and green)
 - 2 Resistances (10,000 and 300 ohms)
 - some cables to connect everything
